A bit more about me


你好! Hello!

I'm a third-year at the University of Waterloo, studying Global Business and Digital Arts (GBDA) and perusing a minor in the Business, Entrepreneurship, and Technology program (BET). Essentially this translates too: someone who loves to tinker at complex business problems in ambiguous environments and derive solutions using design thinking.

Aside from all that, I spend my free time researching my next travel destination, exploring new restaurants (phone always eats first), binge watching YouTube, or trying my hand at digital painting.

So… Why do I design?
My passion for art developed when I was a little kid. Moving every 3-4 years was exciting but also difficult, so I turned to drawing as my go-to mood booster. This eventually became more than just a passion when I discovered the field of HCI. I was fascinated by how design, technology, and business intertwines with the mindset of putting the user first to redefine the possibilities. The industry also allows me to continuously develop my interests; whether that be honing my visual craft, understanding the jargon of code, or bringing diversified perspectives to the table.

”A business designer has the head of a business person, the heart of a designer, and the ability to understand and communicate across the full lifecycle of a product.” – InVisionApp
