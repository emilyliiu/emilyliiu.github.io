A bit more about me


A few fun facts: longest I’ve lived in the same house was 3 years, been to all 10 provinces of Canada, and all 50 states in the USA. Raised in 9 different cities across China and Canada I can confidently say I have a diverse background, am open-minded, and can establish relationships with people from all over the world. Personality type ENFJ, essentially translating to: I am extroverted for the most part, have genuine interest and optimism in others, am understanding and empathetic of another perspective, and loves intuition in problem solving.
