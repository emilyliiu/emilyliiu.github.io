Nice to e-meet you! How's it going?

I'm currently in my third year at the University of Waterloo, studying Global Business and Digital Arts (GBDA) and perusing a minor in Business, Entrepreneurship, and Technology (BET).

Essentially this translates too: someone who tinkers at complex business problems using design and technology in ambiguous environments.

Aside from all that, I enjoy spending my free time travelling, exploring new restaurants (phone always eats first), binge watching YouTube, and trying my hand at digital paintings.

So… Why do I design?
My passion for art developed when I was a little kid. Moving every 2-4 years was exciting but also difficult, so I turned to drawing as my go-to mood booster. Fast forward a few years, I somehow found myself perusing a degree in my passion, something I feel very fortunate to be able to say. Over the years, I learnt how important of a role design (especially behavioural design) is when it comes to understanding the customer and redefining the possibilities.
