Overview

I thought what better way to improve my design skills and familarize myself with various trends than to practice designing more. Promted by Daily UI, I spend Summer '19 creating a simple landing page everyday for 100 days.

Wanting to apply my skills practically, each design is crafted for an existing company. Treating each Daily UI as a client meant I got to explore a branding guideline while learning about a new company each day, satisfying both my interest in business and passion in design.
