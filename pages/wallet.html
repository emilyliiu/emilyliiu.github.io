Apple Wallet Redesign



Breaking down and recreating the Wallet experience.

Toolkit:

  • Photoshop
  • Figma
  • Illustrator

Overview

Apple Wallet has completely replaced my actual wallet. However, despite being one of the most convenient and useful apps, I had dificulty completing a few basic tasks. Out of curiousity, I asked around to see if other users felt the same as I did. I asked around 15-20 friends, co-workers, and family members and concluded the following pain points:

Pain points:

Unable to select default payment card

Apple Wallet payment options appear in chronological order, which adds an extra step if the user's preferred card is not the most recently added card.

Difficult navigation between all the stored cards/passes

Some users suggested a grid-view layout over the up/down swipe, however, other users loved the swiping between cards feature.

Confusing to find a specific pass

Users found the tiny preview difficult to view, especially when there were multiple airplane passes or cards of the same color.
