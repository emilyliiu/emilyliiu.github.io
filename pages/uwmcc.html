Background

The University of Waterloo Management Consulting Club (UWMCC) aims to help students acquire the skills needed to succeed in pursuing opportunities in the consulting industry. With a love for problem solving, I joined the exec team and have been: Marketing Coordinator, VP Marketing, Case Committee, and Advisor.

Why's this in my Portfolio?

I felt these showcased my improvement in Photoshop and Illustrator throughout the past 2 years and highlights my ability to research and design for a specfic industry.

The Problem:

Although UWMCC events are well-known on campus, I felt our marketing materials lacked branding and execution process due to:

  • Color palet felt outdated and unwelcoming
  • Inconsistency in all graphic deliverables
  • Misconception of what information is most important

Solution:

Referencing brand guidelines from MBB, Big 4, and asking around for feedback I decided to:

  • Explore various saturations and brightness of teal
  • Develope hierarchy by leveraging typography and whitespace
